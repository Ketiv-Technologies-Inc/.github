## Who we are

KETIV is a digital-manufacturing solutions provider, integrator, and consultancy. We work with discrete manufacturers — OEMs, equipment builders, and producers across aerospace, food, steel, and industrial — to connect the **digital thread** from sales through design, simulation, and the shop floor.

We don't just resell software. We integrate it, automate it, and stand behind it.

## What we work on

| Practice area | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **CPQ** | Sales automation — configure, price, and quote complex products without leaving the engineering rules behind. |
| **Engineering Simulation** | Replace physical prototyping with credible virtual testing. |
| **Data Management (PDM/PLM)** | The engineering-to-organization data foundation. |
| **Design & Engineering Automation** | Streamline design and validation work that used to be manual. |
| **Advanced Manufacturing** | CAM, CNC, additive, and robotics for the shop floor. |

Each practice has a paired **Solutions** track and **Consulting** offering — software plus the experts who know how to deploy it.

## Products we build

- **[KETIV DataBridge](https://ketiv.com/databridge)** — middleware that synchronizes product and manufacturing data across CAD, ERP, PDM, and CPQ. The integration layer for the digital thread.
- **[Autodesk Vault in the Cloud by KETIV](https://ketiv.com/vault-in-the-cloud)** — managed, cloud-hosted Autodesk Vault PDM. The Vault you know, without the on-prem infrastructure.

## For developers

This organization hosts the code behind KETIV's integrations, automation, and customer-facing tooling. Most repos fall into one of three buckets:

- **DataBridge connectors** — adapters between CAD, ERP, PDM, and CPQ systems.
- **Autodesk automation** — Inventor / AutoCAD / Vault plugins, iLogic patterns, Forge / APS samples.
- **Engineering services tooling** — internal CLIs, deployment automation, and customer enablement code.

### How we work

- **Clean architecture and SOLID** by default, not by ceremony.
- **Tests close to the code** — unit + integration, with realistic fixtures over heavy mocking.
- **CI on every push** via the [reusable workflow templates](../workflow-templates/) in this repo.
- **CodeQL security scanning** enabled org-wide.
- **Conventional commits** to keep release notes honest.
